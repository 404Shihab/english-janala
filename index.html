Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
Is this website free to use?
Yes, most of our lessons and exercises are free. Some advanced courses or features may require a premium subscription.
Do I need to create an account?
You can explore some lessons without an account, but creating an account allows you to track your progress, save lessons, and access more features.
How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, using flashcards, reading examples, and taking vocabulary quizzes available on the website.
Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, after successfully completing certain courses and passing the final assessments, you can receive a certificate of completion.
